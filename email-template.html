Workshop Topic

Building Immersive Digital Twins with AI Integration

This hands-on workshop teaches participants how to build immersive, interactive 3D training environments, known as Digital Twins, with AI integration, without requiring any programming experience. Using tools like Blender, Unity, and Hyperskill, attendees will model physical objects, add animations, create no-code interaction flows, and integrate AI features such as Q&A assistants and real-time feedback agents.

The workshop also covers VR optimization, AI-driven image recognition, and converting standard procedures into live simulations, with semiconductor manufacturing as an example use case.

By the end, participants will understand the Training Content Capture, Creation, and Customization (TC4) process and walk away with a reusable Digital Twin prototype and access to ongoing learning resources.
